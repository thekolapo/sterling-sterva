Frequently Asked Questions
This is a list popular questions customers have asked us about Sterva. If you still can't find what you are looking for, kindly contact us:
How do I register for the service?
Step 1: Simply download the app from the respective stores

Step 2: Register your profile with your mobile number

Step 3: Add your NUBAN (for Sterling Bank customers) or Debit Card (for non-Sterling Bank account holders)

Step 4: Add your preferred chat channel(s)
In the event of a dispute, how do I channel my complaint?
You can have a live chat with one of our Customer Care Agents by selecting the ChatPay menu which is “Chat with us” on your preferred chat channel.
What are the services Sterva offer?
You can check your balance, transfer funds, purchase airtime, pay bills. You can also have a live Chat with an Agent, locate the closest Sterling ATMs around you, purchase movie ticket, change / reset your PIN.
Do I need to always use the app?
No. Once the initial registration is completed using the app, you can always initiate transactions through any of the social media chat platforms you have registered on.
What are the available chat platforms?
Currently, Sterva can assist you on Twitter, Facebook Messenger, Whatsapp, WeChat…
What are Sterva’s transaction limits?
For bank accounts – N100,000 per day.
What are the type of accounts that can be linked to Sterva?
You can link personal Savings and Current accounts.
What are the charges for transactions?
Bill Payment : ₦100

Funds Transfer (other banks) : ₦105

Sterling to Sterling Transfers : ₦0

Airtime Top-Up : ₦0
Can I have access to Sterva if I do not have an account with Sterling Bank?
Yes, non–Sterling Bank account holders can enjoy Sterva’s services by linking their debit card issued by other Nigerian Banks.
How safe are transactions handled by Sterva?
Upon initial registration, an OTP is generated to validate customer information. This is inputted to complete registration process.

For financial transactions, you are required to input a 4-digit PIN which must have been created upon registration before transaction can be processed.

For the individual chat channels, your login credentials act as extra security

For debit card holders, CVV2 and card PIN are required upon registration. These parameters are used by the issuing bank to validate the customer.

Please note that Sterva does not store card information.
Can I register for more than one social chat platform?
Yes, you can register as many chat platforms as available on the app.
Where can I download Sterva?
Android – Play Store. Others are being worked on and will be deployed soon. This will be duly communicated.
Can I link multiple cards and accounts to Sterva?
You can only link a debit card or a bank account to a profile at the moment.
